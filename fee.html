TRANSPARENT LEGAL FEE OPTIONS

Expert Legal Services in pakistan | Naich Law Firm



Legal Consultation: We are pleased to offer expert legal advice on standard hourly fee. This allows you to benefit from our experienced lawyers' insights without a significant upfront commitment.

At Naich Law Firm, we provide the following fee options to cater to your specific requirements: 

1. FLAT FEE:

Tailored for cases lasting up to 6 months, our Flat Fee option provides a fixed cost structure for a variety of legal services, ensuring transparency and budget certainty. This includes drafting legal documents, court appearances, research, and more.

This Flat Fee option extends to a wide range of legal services and cases with a duration of up to 6 months, offering you streamlined support.

Please note that cases with longer durations are not eligible for this option. For such situations, our Retainer or Hourly Fee options are available, providing flexibility and personalized solutions to accommodate your unique needs.

2. RETAINER FEE: 

For individual clients, embark on a journey of legal empowerment starting from an annual fee of USD 3,475 or PKR 1,000,000 for 1 case, with additional services carefully curated to enhance your legal experience.

For businesses, our specialized Retainer offerings are customized to your unique needs, ensuring comprehensive support and seamless navigation through complex legal landscapes. Contact us to explore the full spectrum of our retainer packages and take a step towards empowered legal solutions.

Please note that this option is not available for cases with shorter durations, such as those lasting 6 months or less. For cases of shorter duration, we offer the Flat Fee option exclusively.

3. HOURLY FEE: 

When considering the average annual hours, a non-complex case typically requires around 10 billable hours annually. Our standard hourly rate is USD 350. To begin, an initial deposit of 5 hours is required, followed by subsequent deposits. Transparent invoicing details specific billable activities, including drafting legal documents, court appearances, research, and more.

Please note that the Hourly Fee option is not available for cases with shorter durations, such as those lasting 6 months or less. For cases of shorter duration, we offer the Flat Fee option exclusively. It's important to clarify that this Hourly Fee option is exclusively designed for our international clients who are familiar with and prefer hourly billing methods.



Please be aware that court fees and related expenses are not included in our professional fee. These expenses will be the responsibility of the client.
